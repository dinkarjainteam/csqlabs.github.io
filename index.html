We are an agentic AI company building the world's first autonomous research workforce. Our goal is not just to automate tasks, but to replicate high-level research reasoning. To do this, we need the "Gold Standard." We are looking for veterans who have lived through the evolution of the industry—from telephone units to mobile ethnography—to serve as the Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Experts who will grade, correct, and shape the behavior of our AI agents.

The Role:
You will not be running studies. You will be the Auditor and Mentor to our AI agents. Your job is to review the output generated by our models (analyses of key business questions around social conversations about brand attributes) and rigorously evaluate them against the quality standards of top-tier global consultancies, especially in the space of social listening.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Forensic Evaluation: Review agent outputs (e.g., classification of social verbatim based on topic, relevancy) and score them on logic, bias, accuracy, and commercial viability.
  • Gap Analysis: Identify why the AI failed. Did it miss a cultural nuance? Did it confuse correlation with causation? Did it ask a leading question?
  • Ground Truth Creation: Create "perfect" examples and documentation of specific research tasks to feed into our training datasets (few-shot prompting).
  • Engineering Feedback Loop: Translate "analyst intuition" into structured feedback that our Engineering and ML teams can use to fine-tune the models.

  • Big Agency Pedigree: Proven tenure at top-tier firms (e.g., BCG, Kantar, Sprinklr, etc.) or client-side experience at Fortune 500 brands.
  • Uncompromising Standards: You are the person who used to catch the errors that junior researchers missed. You have a reputation for being a "tough grader" or "bar raiser."
Why Join Us?
